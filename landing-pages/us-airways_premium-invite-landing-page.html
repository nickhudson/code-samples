Important Details About
Upgrading Your Card

Upgrading your US Airways Dividend Miles® MasterCard® account is optional. When you call us at (800) 307-9633 by December 1, 2014, to upgrade your account, your upgrade will be automatically effective as of January 31, 2015, provided you keep your account open, active and in good standing.

Review FAQS

Changes to your Account Terms if you choose to upgrade:

If you request to upgrade your account, the following changes to the terms of your Cardmember Agreement, and also the Rewards Program terms, will apply. Except as described below, the terms of your Cardmember Agreement remain in effect. If you do not upgrade your account, these amendments, including the changes to the Reward terms, will not apply.

Annual Fee

If you upgrade your account, your annual fee will be $195. This change will take effect on your 2015 anniversary date.

You have the right to cancel your upgrade request and reject the changes in this notice by calling us at (800) 307-9633 by
December 15, 2014.




Foreign Transaction Fee

Effective as of January 31, 2015, your account will not be assessed a transaction fee if you use your card to make a Foreign Transaction.

CHANGES TO YOUR REWARD RULES

If you elect to upgrade your account, the reward changes summarized below will apply effective as of January 31, 2015. Please see the Reward Rules that you received in your upgrade package for additional details regarding the benefits.

  • Earn 3X miles US Airways and American Airlines Purchases, 2X miles on hotel and car rental Purchases and 1X miles on all other Purchases
  • First bag checked free on eligible bags for you and up to 8 companions on domestic US Airways operated flights
  • A Companion Ticket good for up to 2 guests at $99 each will be issued each year after your account anniversary, when you spend $30,000 or more in Purchases by your account anniversary date (taxes and fees apply). For 2015, the Companion Certificate will be fulfilled to eligible Cardmembers with an anniversary date after the Frequent Flyer Programs are combined.
  • Earn 5,000 AAdvantage® Elite Qualifying Miles for each $20,000 you spend on Purchases each calendar year (up to 10,000 Elite Qualifying Miles per year). For the 2015 calendar year EQMs earned from this benefit will be posted to your AAdvantage® account once the Frequent Flyer Programs are combined.
  • Priority boarding
  • 25% in-flight savings on in-flight food, beverage and headset purchases on US Airways and American Airlines operated flights

In addition, when the US Airways and American Airlines frequent flyer programs are combined in 2015, you will also begin to enjoy 10% of your redeemed miles back for award flights (10,000 maximum miles per year).

Please note: By choosing to upgrade to the AAdvantage® Aviator™ Silver World Elite MasterCard® with a $195 Annual Fee, you will no longer receive the $100 Flight Discount at Account Anniversary when you spend $30,000 or more with your card.

Please review the information that you received with your upgrade package to ensure the new product suits your needs. If, after reviewing the information, you wish to upgrade your account, please call Customer Care at (800) 307-9633 by December 1, 2014 to accept this special offer. If you subsequently change your mind and no longer want the AAdvantage® Aviator™ Silver World Elite MasterCard®, please call Barclaycard Customer Care at (800) 307-9633 by December 15, 2014.

We appreciate your business and look forward to continuing to service your financial needs. Should you have any questions, we're available at USAirwaysMasterCard.com or at (800) 307-9633.

IF I UPGRADE TO THE AADVANTAGE® AVIATOR™ SILVER WORLD ELITE MASTERCARD®, WHEN WILL THE NEW BENEFITS BE AVAILABLE?

All the benefits will be available to you effective January 2015 with the exception of the 10% of your redeemed miles back benefit and the companion certificate, which will be available once the frequent flyer programs are combined. Please see the Reward Rules that you received as part of your upgrade package for additional details regarding these benefits.

WHEN WILL I RECEIVE MY NEW AADVANTAGE® AVIATOR™ SILVER WORLD ELITE MASTERCARD®?

You will receive your new card after the two frequent flyer programs are combined, retaining the same card account number as you have today. Until then, keep using your current US Airways Dividend Miles® MasterCard®. Though you won’t have the new card in hand, you will begin to enjoy the benefits that your new AAdvantage® Aviator™ Silver World Elite MasterCard® has to offer effective January 2015.

WHEN WILL I BE BILLED THE ANNUAL FEE?

If you upgrade your account to the AAdvantage® Aviator™ Silver World Elite MasterCard® your annual fee of $195 will be billed to your account on your 2015 anniversary date. Until then, enjoy the new benefits with our compliments.
